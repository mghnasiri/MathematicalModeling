# Job Shop Scheduling — Literature

## Foundational Papers

- Roy, B. & Sussmann, B. (1964). "Les Problèmes d'Ordonnancement avec Contraintes Disjonctives" — SEMA, Note D.S. No. 9
  > Introduces the disjunctive graph representation.

- Garey, M.R., Johnson, D.S. & Sethi, R. (1976). "The Complexity of Flowshop and Jobshop Scheduling" — *Mathematics of Operations Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/moor.1.2.117)
  > Proves NP-hardness of $J_2 \mid\mid C_{\max}$.

- Giffler, B. & Thompson, G.L. (1960). "Algorithms for Solving Production Scheduling Problems" — *Operations Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.8.4.487)
  > G&T algorithm for generating active schedules.

- Adams, J., Balas, E. & Zawack, D. (1988). "The Shifting Bottleneck Procedure for Job Shop Scheduling" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.34.3.391)
  > The shifting bottleneck heuristic.

## Key Algorithmic Papers

- Nowicki, E. & Smutnicki, C. (1996). "A Fast Taboo Search Algorithm for the Job Shop Problem" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.42.6.797)
  > TSAB — tabu search with N5 neighborhood.

- Nowicki, E. & Smutnicki, C. (2005). "An Advanced Tabu Search Algorithm for the Job Shop Problem" — *Journal of Scheduling* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10951-005-2861-2)
  > i-TSAB — improved version, state-of-the-art for years.

- Bierwirth, C. (1995). "A Generalized Permutation Approach to Job Shop Scheduling with Genetic Algorithms" — *OR Spektrum* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01719250)
  > Permutation-based GA encoding for JSP.

- Balas, E. & Vazacopoulos, A. (1998). "Guided Local Search with Shifting Bottleneck for Job Shop Scheduling" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.44.2.262)

- Applegate, D. & Cook, W. (1991). "A Computational Study of the Job-Shop Scheduling Problem" — *ORSA Journal on Computing* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/ijoc.3.2.149)
  > First optimal solution of ft10.

## Constraint Programming

- Baptiste, P., Le Pape, C. & Nuijten, W. (2001). *Constraint-Based Scheduling* — [Springer](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4615-1479-4)

- Da Col, G. & Teppan, E.C. (2022). "Google CP-SAT for Job Shop Scheduling" — Various computational results showing CP-SAT competitiveness
