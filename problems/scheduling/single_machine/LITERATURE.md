# Single Machine Scheduling — Literature

## Foundational Papers

- Smith, W.E. (1956). "Various Optimizers for Single-Stage Production" — *Naval Research Logistics Quarterly* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1002/nav.3800030106)
  > Introduces the SPT and WSPT rules.

- Moore, J.M. (1968). "An n Job, One Machine Sequencing Algorithm for Minimizing the Number of Late Jobs" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.15.1.102)
  > Moore's algorithm for $1 \mid\mid \sum U_j$.

- Du, J. & Leung, J.Y.-T. (1990). "Minimizing Total Tardiness on One Machine is NP-Hard" — *Mathematics of Operations Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/moor.15.3.483)
  > Proves NP-hardness of $1 \mid\mid \sum T_j$.

- Lawler, E.L. (1977). "A Pseudopolynomial Algorithm for Sequencing Jobs to Minimize Total Tardiness" — *Annals of Discrete Mathematics* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/S0167-5060(08)70742-8)
  > DP algorithm for total tardiness.

## Key Algorithmic Papers

- Potts, C.N. & Van Wassenhove, L.N. (1985). "A Branch and Bound Algorithm for the Total Weighted Tardiness Problem" — *Operations Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.33.2.363)

- Congram, R.K., Potts, C.N. & van de Velde, S.L. (2002). "An Iterated Dynasearch Algorithm for the Single-Machine Total Weighted Tardiness Scheduling Problem" — *INFORMS Journal on Computing* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/ijoc.14.1.52.7712)

- Vepsalainen, A.P.J. & Morton, T.E. (1987). "Priority Rules for Job Shops with Weighted Tardiness Costs" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.33.8.1035)
  > Introduces the Apparent Tardiness Cost (ATC) rule.
