# Parallel Machine Scheduling — Literature

## Foundational Papers

- McNaughton, R. (1959). "Scheduling with Deadlines and Loss Functions" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.6.1.1)
  > Optimal preemptive scheduling on parallel machines.

- Graham, R.L. (1969). "Bounds on Multiprocessing Timing Anomalies" — *SIAM J. on Applied Mathematics* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1137/0117039)
  > LPT analysis — the 4/3 bound.

- Hochbaum, D.S. & Shmoys, D.B. (1987). "Using Dual Approximation Algorithms for Scheduling Problems: Theoretical and Practical Results" — *JACM* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1145/7531.7535)
  > PTAS for $P_m \mid\mid C_{\max}$.

- Coffman, E.G., Garey, M.R. & Johnson, D.S. (1978). "An Application of Bin-Packing to Multiprocessor Scheduling" — *SIAM J. on Computing* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1137/0207001)
  > MULTIFIT algorithm.

## Key Algorithmic Papers

- Dell'Amico, M. & Martello, S. (1995). "Optimal Scheduling of Tasks on Identical Parallel Processors" — *ORSA Journal on Computing* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/ijoc.7.2.191)

- Lenstra, J.K., Shmoys, D.B. & Tardos, É. (1990). "Approximation Algorithms for Scheduling Unrelated Parallel Machines" — *Mathematical Programming* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01585178)
  > 2-approximation for $R_m \mid\mid C_{\max}$.

- Shchepin, E.V. & Vakhania, N. (2005). "An Optimal Rounding Gives a Better Approximation for Scheduling on Unrelated Machines" — *Operations Research Letters* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.orl.2004.05.004)
