# RCPSP — Literature

## Foundational Papers

- Kelley, J.E. & Walker, M.R. (1959). "Critical-Path Planning and Scheduling" — *Proceedings of Eastern Joint Computer Conference*
  > Origin of CPM — the precursor to RCPSP.

- Pritsker, A.A.B., Watters, L.J. & Wolfe, P.M. (1969). "Multiproject Scheduling with Limited Resources: A Zero-One Programming Approach" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.16.1.93)
  > Early MIP formulation for RCPSP.

- Kolisch, R. & Sprecher, A. (1997). "PSPLIB — A Project Scheduling Problem Library" — *European Journal of Operational Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/S0377-2217(96)00170-1)
  > Defines the PSPLIB benchmark.

- Kolisch, R. (1996). "Serial and Parallel Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling Methods Revisited: Theory and Computation" — *European Journal of Operational Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/0377-2217(95)00348-7)
  > Formalizes serial and parallel SGS.

## Key Algorithmic Papers

- Demeulemeester, E. & Herroelen, W. (1992). "A Branch-and-Bound Procedure for the Multiple Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling Problem" — *Management Science* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.38.12.1803)
  > Effective exact algorithm.

- Hartmann, S. (1998). "A Competitive Genetic Algorithm for Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling" — *Naval Research Logistics* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1002/(SICI)1520-6750(199809)45:7<733::AID-NAV5>3.0.CO;2-C)
  > GA with activity-list encoding + serial SGS.

- Merkle, D., Middendorf, M. & Schmeck, H. (2002). "Ant Colony Optimization for Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling" — *IEEE Transactions on Evolutionary Computation* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1109/TEVC.2002.802450)

- Debels, D. et al. (2006). "A Hybrid Scatter Search / Electromagnetism Meta-heuristic for Project Scheduling" — *European Journal of Operational Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ejor.2005.04.015)

## Surveys & Comprehensive Reviews

- Kolisch, R. & Hartmann, S. (2006). "Experimental Investigation of Heuristics for Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling: An Update" — *European Journal of Operational Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ejor.2004.12.006)
  > The definitive computational comparison of RCPSP heuristics.

- Hartmann, S. & Briskorn, D. (2022). "An Updated Survey of Variants and Extensions of the Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling Problem" — *European Journal of Operational Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ejor.2021.05.004)

- Herroelen, W. & Leus, R. (2005). "Project Scheduling Under Uncertainty: Survey and Research Potentials" — *European Journal of Operational Research* — [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ejor.2004.04.002)
