Page Moved

The Smart Grid Routing page has been replaced by Optimal Power Flow (OPF) as part of the 2026 restructure.

The original page solved a shortest-path problem on an abstract demand grid — a commodity minimum-cost flow, not grid physics. OPF is the canonical power-systems problem: minimize generation cost subject to Kirchhoff's laws, line thermal limits, voltage bounds, and generator limits. The new page covers DC-OPF (linearized), AC-OPF (nonconvex), and SOCP / SDP relaxations, following Carpentier (1962), Cain, O'Neill & Castillo (2012), and Low (2014).
