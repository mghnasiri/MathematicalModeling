Page Moved

The Transmission Network Design page has been replaced by Transmission Expansion Planning (TEP) as part of the 2026 restructure.

The original page solved a maximum-flow problem on a fixed network. TEP is a different problem: it decides which new lines to build (binary investments) over a multi-year horizon to integrate new generation, eliminate congestion, and meet reliability (N−1) criteria — coupled with a DC-OPF routing subproblem. Formulation follows Garver (1970) and the review of Hemmati, Hooshmand & Khodabakhshian (2013).
